# 1.0 export surface — decision record

> **Status: signed (RC-1).** This record names the exact public surface Fundamental commits to at 1.0,
> and the tier of every public symbol. **No "to-add before 1.0" item remains open** (the RC-1 / Phase
> A-1 predicate, gate spec §0.1 / §3.1 — closes #316, #318). The machine-checked source of truth for the
> frozen tier is `scripts/api-surface.data.mjs` (CI-enforced by `check:api`); this document is the
> human-readable companion that also declares the *additive-stable* and *experimental* tiers.

The surface has three tiers. **Stable** (frozen + additive-stable) is the 1.0 stability promise: it
will not break within `1.x` (additions only). **Experimental** ships but is explicitly excluded from the
promise and may change in a `1.x` minor. **Out of 1.0** is not shipped.

## Tier 1 — Frozen (the 1.0 contract, CI-enforced)

The 13 entries in `scripts/api-surface.data.mjs`, plus the `[data-body="…"]` authoring contract:

- **Entry points:** `createField` (core, host-required) · `createField` (vanilla, host-bundled) ·
  `createFieldPlatform` · `applyRecipe` · `bindData` · `browserHost` · `compileRecipe`
- **Types:** `FieldHost` · `FieldPlatform` · `FieldRecipe`
- **Elements:** `<field-root>` (also `<field-field>`) · `<field-cell>`
- **Contract:** `[data-body]` tokens, the `--field-*` feedback variables, the `data-*` attributes.

Renaming or removing any of these is a breaking change. ✅ Already gated.

## Tier 2 — Additive-stable (promoted to the 1.0 promise)

Shipped, tested, mirrored across vanilla / `<field-root>` / React / three. Committed at 1.0:

- **`FieldHandle` surface:** `scan` · `rescan` · `setAccent` · `setPalette` · `setFormation` ·
  `setAttention` · `setCausality` · `setHeatmap` · `setBackground` · `setDprCap` · `setQualityTier` ·
  `setRender` · `setOverlay` · `threads` · `burst` · `flowTo` · `clearFlow` · `seed` · `addAgent` ·
  `addBody` · `addField` · `sampleField` · `atomAt` · `focusAt` · `clearFocus` · `particleCount` ·
  `readParticles` · `readParticleIds` · `energy` · `sample` · `sampleScalar` · `sampleGradient` ·
  `grid` · `on` (events: `absorb` · `release` · `enter` · `exit` · `met`) · `scrollV` · `setVisible` ·
  `version` · `destroy` · `.handle` (elements).
- **`FieldOptions`:** `accent` · `palette` · `theme` · `gradientCool` · `gradientWarm` · `waveBaseline` ·
  `density` · `render` · `waves` · `background` · `mass` · `attention` · `causality` · `heatmap` ·
  `overlay` · `gridWarp` · `gridIntensity` · `dprCap` · `depth` · `host` · `bounds`.
- **Exports:** `containerHost` · `createBrowserField` · `FieldField` · `mountField` · `useFieldField` ·
  `FIELD_VERSION` · `THEMES` · `FieldTheme` · `cssFeedbackSink` · `FORCES` · `FORMATIONS` · `CONDITIONS`
  · `PALETTE` · `FIELD_RECIPES`.

## Tier 3 — Experimental (shipped, NOT under the 1.0 promise)

May change or be removed in a `1.x` minor; documented as experimental:

- Performance budget: `inspectBudget` · `DEFAULT_BUDGET` · `createFieldPerf` · `QualityGovernor`.
- Measured thermodynamics (`--entropy` / `--coherence` / `--temperature`), the `screen` quiet-zone
  modifier, the advanced diagnostic render/overlay modes, and the platform lint internals.
- `EXPERIMENTAL_RECIPES` (distinct from the frozen 64-recipe catalog).

## Out of 1.0 (planned / not shipped)

GPU / WebGPU backend · custom render backends · multi-root bridge · AI evidence fields · the visual
recipe editor. These carry no 1.0 commitment and are tracked separately.
